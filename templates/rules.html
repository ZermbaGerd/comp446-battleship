You have 5 ships. They are precious. Your opponent also has 5 ships, which are evil and must be destroyed. They, however, will seek to destroy your precious 5 ships! End them.

Setup

Before the game starts, you'll have to place your ships onto the board. Click a ship on the small board to the left to pick it up. Then, click the square you want to place its endpoint onto on the big board to the right. This will show you where you can place it, as pictured below. Click any of the purple highlighted tiles to finish placing it.

You can press Escape to stop placing the ship. You can also select a ship you've already placed to move it somewhere else. Once the game starts, players will take turns. Once it's your turn, click a tile on the rightmost board to attack it! That will be your turn. You can see what your opponent has attacked via the board on the left.

Keyboard Controls

Ship Placement

Use number keys (1-5) to pick up ships. Use arrows to navigate the placement board. Tiles will be highlighted aqua while selected (). Press enter to begin placing the ship, and then an arrow to indicate what direction you want it to go. You can press Escape at any time to de-select a ship. Press Ctrl+Enter when you're ready to start the game.

Ingame Controls

Use arrows to navigate your opponent's board. Tiles will be highlighted aqua while selected (). Press enter to lock in your guess. On your next turn, your keyboard cursor will remain wherever it last was.
