The Rules of Warfare

You have 5 ships. They are precious. Your opponent also has 5 ships, which are evil and must be destroyed. They, however, will seek to destroy your precious 5 ships! End them.

Before the game starts, you'll have to place your ships onto the board. Click a ship on the left to pick it up, and then click the squares you want to place it onto on the board on the right. Once the game starts, players will take turns. Once it's your turn, click a tile on the rightmost board to attack it! That will be your turn. You can see what your opponent has attacked via the board on the left.
